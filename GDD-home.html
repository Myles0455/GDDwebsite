Game Development and Design

About

At GDD we focus on collaboration between programmers and artists to create games using the same tools as professional developers. We need artists, musicians, programmers, and writers to bring these projects to life! Best of all, no experience required. We’re here for experts to noobies and everything in between, whether you have a project you want to work on or are just interested in learning.

"In addition, we have a variety of local professional developers and artists who come speak to us, answer questions, and to tell us about their real world experiences. If you're looking for professional connections and resources in Game Development and Design, this is the club for you!
(If it isn't obvious, we’re really proud of our club)

Keep an eye for this year's changes. For starters, our name change from GDC to GDD
to branch Game Development to all aspects of Design, and to be more inclusive to our
wonderful artists. In addition, watch out for our new logos, soon to be available as
stickers. Our club this semester will focus on our fantastic community and professional
development. If you're looking for an avenue to explore professional Game Development and
Design while being apart of a supportive community, come check us out!

To follow us on social media, to join our emailing list, or to send us an email, go to our Contact Us page.

We look forward to meeting you!
